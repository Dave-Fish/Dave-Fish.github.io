Planning

Participants would hold a sphere with a texture in front of them
Press a button when optimum distance away

Issues with this plan:

Doesn't take up much of the participant's view,
What we'd be looking at is if the sphere as a whole is smaller or larger,
Participants are likely to physically recoil from particularly bad textures,
Difficulty wrapping texture around a sphere but sharp edges mess with the over all spatial frequency,
The angle the viewer looks at the sphere massively affects the spatial frequency,
Could keep the texture position relative to the viewport but difficulties implementing and takes away from the purpose of using VR
