Catboy Obama
My first discord bot. I made him to schedule my household, so that each morning he would send a message asking us to vote on whether we wanted to eat food together tonight. We could choose home cooked meal, takeaway, or do our own thing. If we all selected home cooked meal, he would follow up by asking us to vote on who should cook. He has a few more functions now, like regularly updating a friend group's server's icon. He's my basic personal utility bot.
